About Us

Welcome to Pakhlay

At Pakhlay, we bring you fresh, homemade lunches prepared with care in a clean and hygienic kitchen. From freshly ground masalas to diet-conscious recipes, our meals are made to keep you healthy, energized, and satisfied during your workday.

Whether you are an individual looking for a one-time lunch or an office team that needs a monthly package, we have flexible plans to meet your needs. With on-time delivery across Islamabad, you can also choose to pick up your meal directly from us.

At Pakhlay, there’s no compromise on quality. Our focus is on cleanliness, balanced flavors, and consistent service – because your health and trust matter the most to us.
